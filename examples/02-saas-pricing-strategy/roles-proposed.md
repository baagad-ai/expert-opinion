# Proposed Expert Roles — SaaS Pricing Strategy Memo

The following 7 expert perspectives have been identified as most relevant to this artifact.
Each expert will investigate specific aspects of the submission in parallel,
then findings will be synthesized into a prioritized audit document.

---

**Business Strategist**
*Go-to-market strategy, competitive positioning, revenue model design*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- Does the seat-based model align with how Vantara's actual buyers (e-commerce ops teams) think about value, or does it create friction at the buying decision?
- Is the $29 Starter tier positioned to convert prospects or primarily to win back leads lost to Databox's free tier — and are those the same customer segment?
- What is the long-term moat if Klipfolio or Databox can match the $29 entry price within 2 quarters?

---

**Pricing Analyst**
*Pricing model mechanics, price-volume tradeoffs, willingness-to-pay research*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- Is there willingness-to-pay data supporting the $29/$99/$299 price points, or are they derived from competitive anchoring alone?
- Usage-based pricing was "rejected due to billing infrastructure" — is this a strategic rejection or an execution constraint that should be revisited given the competitive landscape?
- What is the modeled impact on ARPU if Growth-tier adoption is lower than projected and most paid conversions land on Starter?

---

**Customer Success Manager**
*Customer retention, churn dynamics, onboarding & activation*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- Is 90 days sufficient for engaged free users (320 accounts) to evaluate, negotiate, and receive budget approval for a paid plan, particularly at mid-market accounts with procurement cycles?
- What is the risk that the concentrated 90-day cliff creates a support surge and a reputational event (social media backlash, G2/Capterra reviews) that damages brand trust during the pricing launch window?
- Does the $9/mo migration offer for 6 months create a cliff at month 7 where those users churn en masse?

---

**Competitive Intelligence Analyst**
*Competitor behavior, market positioning, response simulation*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- Databox's 18% free-to-paid conversion rate is cited — what is the source and how comparable is their free-tier cohort to Vantara's 320 engaged free users?
- Is there a scenario where sunsetting the free tier triggers Databox to aggressively target Vantara's free-user base with a migration campaign before Vantara's grace period ends?
- The memo positions below Klipfolio's entry price — but what happens to this moat if Klipfolio launches a Starter tier in response?

---

**CFO / Finance Lens**
*Unit economics, cash flow impact, revenue projection integrity*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- The 40% ARPU increase projection — what conversion rate assumptions does it require from free-to-paid and existing single-tier users to existing tiers?
- Does the 20% annual discount create a cash-flow benefit that outweighs the revenue-per-user reduction, and at what annual plan adoption rate does this break even?
- Is the $29 Starter tier actually below CAC-to-LTV target as stated — and if so, what is the modeled payback period, and how many Starter users are expected to upgrade to Growth within 12 months?

---

**Product Manager**
*Feature gating strategy, tier differentiation, product-led growth mechanics*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- Are the features gated at each tier creating genuine "aha moment" barriers, or are they arbitrary limits that will generate support tickets ("why can't I export my own data on Starter?")?
- With the free tier gone, what is the trial and onboarding funnel — is a free trial offered, and how does it compare to the removed free tier in terms of activation potential?
- Does removing the free tier eliminate the primary acquisition channel for the word-of-mouth and integration-marketplace growth loops that B2B analytics tools rely on?

---

**Legal / Compliance Reviewer**
*Contract terms, consumer protection, data deletion obligations*

Focus questions for this artifact:
- When free accounts are deactivated at month 4, what is the data retention and deletion obligation under GDPR Article 17 (right to erasure) and CCPA for the 2,080 dormant and 320 active free users?
- Does the 90-day grace period email constitute sufficient notice under applicable consumer protection regulations, or do specific jurisdictions (EU, California) require additional disclosure steps?
- Are the Enterprise tier's white-labeling and data export features covered by standard DPA terms, or do they require amended data processing agreements for enterprise customers?
